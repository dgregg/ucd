About Us

Welcome to Outdoor Depot where we share your passion for the outdoors. Like you, the team at Outdoor Depot is comprised of outdoor enthusiasts, explorers and adventurers. That's why we seek out the best stories, the best information and the best clothing and equipment available today. Whatever path you choose, we hope to help you along the way.

The Outdoor Depot Web site launched in March 2001 with the belief that spending time outside - whether climbing a mountain or taking a quiet hike - helps us to live a healthy, balanced life.

The Outdoor Depot management team is well-equipped to serve people's outdoor and active travel lifestyle needs. Our team has extensive experience in the Internet, travel, and retail fields.
